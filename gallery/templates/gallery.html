About

Hi there, I am Santosh Sapkota and Welcome to my webpage.


This photography gallery showcases pictures I have captured over the years. My photography journey began with the first image in this slideshow section, which I posted on Instagram (See here). I was truly amazed and satisfied with the quality and overall vibe of that photo. I couldn’t tell whether it was just the beauty of the flower or my photography skills that made it stand out. Whatever the reason, that moment ignited my passion for photography.


After that, I actively started sharing my photographs on my Instagram account. Since I didn’t travel often, finding new subjects to capture was a challenge, so I turned to YouTube for inspiration. While exploring ideas and photographing whatever was around me, I realized that the camera allowed me to see the world from a different perspective—one that encourages mindfulness, appreciation, and a peaceful awareness of my surroundings. I guess that's why I can still remember the story behind most of my photos and even picture myself taking them. Maybe not every single one, but almost all—saying 100% would be a bit risky!


These images below are one of my best till date, I was and still am proud of capturing those photos with my old camera of Oppo A37f.
